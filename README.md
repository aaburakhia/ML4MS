## üß≠ The Research Radar
*Active investigations at the intersection of AI and Heavy Industry.*

#### 1. Accelerated Discovery & Generative Design
Investigating how Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) and Diffusion Models are creating new alloy compositions for Aerospace and Additive Manufacturing, and how we validate these "dreamed" materials against physical constraints.

#### 2. Physics-Informed Machine Learning (PINNs)
Moving beyond traditional Finite Element Analysis (FEA). We are exploring how neural networks can solve differential equations for corrosion rates, heat transfer, and stress analysis 100x faster than traditional simulation methods.

#### 3. Computer Vision in Characterization
Automating the analysis of SEM (Scanning Electron Microscopy) and metallography images. Using CNNs to detect micro-cracks, calculate phase fractions, and identify failure mechanisms instantly.
